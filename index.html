Apply in 4 Steps

1

Prep

Clean, push back cuticles, lightly buff. Takes 2 minutes.

2

Size

Match each nail to the correct size — every set includes 12 sizes.

3

Apply

Press down firmly from cuticle to tip for 30 seconds.

4

Enjoy

Wear for 2–4 weeks. Remove with warm water — nails stay healthy.
